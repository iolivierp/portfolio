Brain Damaged Space Mechs

What is it ?

It is a 2D top-down space shooter where you need to survive as long as you can in order to achieve the best score.
Dodge the many asteroids coming at you and try to take out as many foes as you can before you get hit !
This project was made by a group of 6 students in 4 days during a special week at the university. We had to make a game in a short period of time with recently aquired C knowledge taught by the teachers of the IUT Annecy.
The idea was to do a Lovers in a Dangerous Spacetime-like since all the members of the group enjoyed cooperative games.
The game is fully playable with a controller.

Date: 2018

Technologies: C, C++, SFML

Brain Damaged Spicy Modern Warfare

What is it ?

It as a classical worms-like games, with two teams of little plastic soldiers, each controlled by a player one turn at a time.
This project was done in 4 days by a group of 5 students during a Unity themed week at the university. We were invited to make a game using Unity and object oriented programming (C#).
We wanted to experiment destructible terrain and turn-base combat, so we thaught that Worms was the perfect project.

Since we wanted to use little green soldiers, we immediately thought of Toy Story, which is why the background image is a drawn reproduction of Andy's room.

The game isn't perfect and there are a few bugs but it's nonetheless really enjoyable with your friends.

Date: 2019

Technologies: C#, Unity

Escalapp (PRO)

What is it ?

During the second year of my studies, we get the chance to make a project for a company.

Our project is to make a website and a Android application to help climbers share their results and compare themselves.

It works like this: every routes have a total of 1000 points and each time someone finish it, they get points relative to the number of users that achieved it.

For example: if someone is the first to accomplish it, he gets a 1000 points, if someone else do it, they both get 500 (the first one loses some of his points).

As a result, the easier the route is, the less points you get by achieving it.

This project is entirely made by 6 students.
