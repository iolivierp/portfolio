Brain Damaged Space Mechs
What is it ?
It is a 2D top-down space shooter where you need to survive as long as you can in order to achieve the best score.
Escape from multiple asteroids coming at you and try to take out as many foes as you can before you get hit !
This project was made by a group of 6 students in 4 days during a special week at the university. We had to make a game in a short period of time with our recently aquired C knowledge taught by the teachers of the IUT Annecy.
The idea was to do a Lovers in a Dangerous Spacetime-like since all the members of the group enjoy cooperative games.
The game is fully playable with a controller.
Date: 2018
Technologies: C, C++, SFML
Brain Damaged Spicy Modern Warfare
What is it ?
Classical worms-like games, with two teams of little plastic soldiers, each controlled by a player one turn at a time.
This project was done in 4 days by a group of 5 students during a Unity theme week at the university. We were invited to make a game using Unity and object oriented programming (C#).
We wanted to experiment destructible terrain and turn base combat, so we thaught that worms was the perfect project.
The game isn't perfect and there are few bugs here and there but it's nonetheless really enjoyable with your friends.
Date: 2019
Technologies: C#, Unity
